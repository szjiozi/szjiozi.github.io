Previously, I completed my Master’s at New York University, where I worked with [Prof. Ashish Sabharwal](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ashish-sabharwal-82a2b661/) and [Prof. Niranjan Balasubramanian](https://www3.cs.stonybrook.edu/~niranjan/index.html).
